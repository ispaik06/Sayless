First launch

macOS may block the app once.

Sayless is distributed outside the Mac App Store. If macOS says it cannot verify the developer, open System Settings and allow it manually.

  1. Open System Settings.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security.
  3. Scroll down and click Open Anyway for Sayless.
  4. Launch Sayless again and grant Accessibility permission when prompted.
